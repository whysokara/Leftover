Common questions

Does Leftover delete photos permanently?
No. Anything you delete goes to your iPhone's own Recently Deleted album for 30 days, same as deleting from the Photos app directly, so it's always recoverable during that window.

Privacy

Leftover is local-only and collects nothing. See the full Privacy Policy.
